03 — How It Works

Large Language Model.
Demystified.

01
Prompt sent
whydocatssleepsomuch?
Text split into tokens — small chunks of words. The model has never "seen" your question before.
→
02
Next token predicted
Billions of parameters trained on trillions of words calculate the most probable next token.
→
03
Response builds up
Not retrieved — generated token by token. Each word shapes the probability of the next.
It doesn't think. It doesn't know. It predicts — very well.
